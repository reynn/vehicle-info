## Ingredients

- 512g flour (all purpose or bread)
- 20g kosher salt
- 10g msg
- 3g thyme
- 8g pepper
- 455g water (use slightly less water with active dry yeast)

## Instructions

- Combine dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl
- Add water and combine with spatula
- Rest covered in fridge 12+ hours
- Rest at room temp 2+ hours
- Pour into oiled baking dish, Dimple surface, add any extras such as flaky/kosher salt, or rosemary/thyme
- Bake 425° ~28m
