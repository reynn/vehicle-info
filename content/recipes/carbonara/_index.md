## Ingredients

- 6 oz Bucatini
- 4 oz Bacon
- 1 shallot
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 cup Heavy Cream
- 2 tablespoons Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
- 4 large eggs (Yolks only)

## Instructions

- Cook noodles
- While noodles are cooking cook bacon until fat has been mostly rendered
- Add shallot and garlic and cook for about 1 minute
- Add cream and simmer until slightly thickened
- Remove from heat and add cheese, egg and reserved pasta water
- Season with Salt and pepper
- Optional: Garnish with Parsley
