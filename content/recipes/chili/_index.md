## Ingredients

### Meats & Veggies

- 1 lb Ground Beef/Chicken/Turkey
- 1 lb Ground Chorizo
- 2 Cans Fire Roasted Diced Tomatoes
- 1 Yellow Onion
- 3-6 large Garlic bulbs
- 2-3 Celery Stalks
- Carrots
- 2-3 Potatoes (Yukon gold works best)

### Spices

- Paprika (smoked sweet is best)
- Cumin
- Cayenne
- Chili Powder
### Liquid

- Gochujang
- Soy Sauce
- Steak Sauce
- Worcestershire Sauce
- Bourbon
- Double shot of Espresso

## Instructions

- Sear meat in cast iron pot, add small amount of dry spices
- Remove meat, cook onion and add garlic.
- Once the garlic is fragrant add the rest of the veggies and canned tomatoes.
- Add some more dry spices and the liquid ingredients to the pot
- Cook on low-medium for 2-6 hours
